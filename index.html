RUSSIAN CIVIL WAR


The Russian Civil War (RCW) was a civil war between the Russian White Army (pro-Empire), and the Bolshevik Red Army (pro-Soviet). It lasted from November 7, 1917 - October 25, 1922. This website aims to provide a decently in-depth history and analysis of the Russian Civil War, and its impact on the world, from pre-civil war to post-civil war.
